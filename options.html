Configure the websites that shall be decorated with a border. You can either supply the full address, or the beginning (a prefix). Alternatively, regular expressions are supported.

See the examples for details.

Address Border Color Border Thickness +
✗ ⇑ ⇓

Examples

Pattern Matches Doesn't match
https://twitter.com Normal (encrypted, HTTPS) Twitter pages Unencrypted Twitter pages, or encrypted pages from a subdomain of Twitter
https://.*\.facebook\.com Any encrypted Facebook page, even from a subdomain. Unencrypted Facebook pages, or pages of another domain.
http://developer.android.com/(training|guide)/.* Training and guides on Android Developers Reference on http://developer.android.com/reference/

You can learn more about regular expressions on http://regexone.com (not affiliated with this extension).
